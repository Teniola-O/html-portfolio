The Best Movies According to Teniola

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Scott Pilgrim vs. the World

Adapted from the graphic novels of the same name. Edgar Wright at his finest.

Charade

Cary Grant and Audrey Hepburn lead comedy with shap dialouge.

Cleo from 5 to 7

French New Wave film following Cleo through Paris as she awaits medical results.

Portrait of a Lady on Fire

A perfect romance movie with stunning visuals.
